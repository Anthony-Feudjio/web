Benefits of Following a Healthy Diet

Explain the benefits of a healthy diet and a fruit-based diet.

Well-Known Fruit and Vegetable-Based Diets:

  • The Mediterranean Diet: Focuses on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein.
  • The DASH Diet: Emphasizes fruits, vegetables, and low-fat dairy.
  • The Plant-Based Diet: Primarily consists of fruits, vegetables, nuts, seeds, and whole grains.
